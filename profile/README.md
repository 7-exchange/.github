## What we're building

7.Exchange routes trades across DEXs and bridges in one continuous flow. Instead of relying on quoted prices alone, routes are selected on **execution quality** — accounting for liquidity depth, slippage, fees, and reliability across multi-step cross-chain paths. All costs are visible upfront, and the system is non-custodial end to end.

Supported trading modes include cross-chain swaps, decentralized limit orders, perpetual aggregation, and direct-deposit trading. Wallet support spans **EVM, Solana, Bitcoin, Sui, and Tron**.
