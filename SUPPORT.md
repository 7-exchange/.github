## Reporting a bug

If you've hit a reproducible bug in one of our repositories, open an issue using the **Bug report** template in the relevant repo. For bugs without a public repo, report in [Discord](https://discord.gg/DArANebGFm).

Please include your environment, the chain(s) involved, transaction hashes where relevant, and clear reproduction steps.

## What this is not for

- **Security vulnerabilities** → [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md)
- **Recovering funds sent to the wrong address** → cross-chain transactions are non-custodial and irreversible; we cannot reverse on-chain transactions
- **Financial or investment advice** → see our [Risk Disclosure](https://7.exchange/risk)
